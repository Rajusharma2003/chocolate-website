Frequently Asked
Questions

What is BonneBelle?

BonneBelle is a premium chocolatier brand specialising in handcrafted and artistic chocolates. We create exquisite treats using the finest ingredients to deliver a truly indulgent experience

Do you deliver pan India?

Currently, we deliver across Delhi NCR. For other areas, please contact us directly to check availability and shipping options.

How can I place an order?

You can place an order through WhatsApp, email, or our social media channels. Simply send us a message with your requirements, and our team will assist you in completing your order seamlessly. For bulk or custom orders, please get in touch via email or phone.

What type of chocolate do you use?

At BonneBelle, we use only premium-quality, imported chocolate to ensure an unparalleled taste experience in every bite

Are BonneBelle chocolates customisable?

Yes, we offer customisation for events like weddings, baby showers, corporate gifting, and festivals. You can choose from our range of flavours, packaging, and personalized branding options.

What is the shelf life of BonneBelle chocolates?

Our chocolates are best enjoyed within 2 to 3 weeks from the date of purchase. To extend their shelf life, store them in an airtight container or place the box in a zip lock bag and refrigerate.

How to store the chocolates to maintain freshness?

To maintain the freshness and flavor of our chocolates, store them in a cool, dry place at a temperature between 18°C and 22°C. Avoid exposing them to heat, humidity, or direct sunlight, as these factors can affect their quality

Do you offer bulk or wholesale orders?

Yes, we provide bulk and wholesale options for retailers, events, and corporate gifting. Please contact us for more details and pricing.

Do you have a physical store?

Currently, BonneBelle operates online. However, we occasionally participate in pop-up events and markets. Follow us on social media for updates on locations and events.
