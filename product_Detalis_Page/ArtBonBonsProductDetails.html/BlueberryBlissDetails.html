Blueberry Bliss

Experience pure blueberry bliss, with a dark chocolate shell, a burst of juicy blueberry filling topped with a smooth and creamy blueberry-infused dark chocolate ganache.

Inquiry Now

Each box is a little celebration.

You can choose from our three options:

  • A box of 5
  • A box of 9
  • A box of 15

You can opt for a box filled with a single flavor or go for an assorted selection.

We also offer customisable flavor combinations to match your mood, preferences or the occasion.

Consume within 2 weeks.

Allergen Info: Contains soy, milk solids, nuts and gluten.

Storage Info: Store in cool, dark place away from sunlight (18-24 degree celcius)
